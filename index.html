EcoLane

A green and technological solution to absorb urban CO₂ emissions

Introduction

EcoLane is a sustainable urban innovation project that combines nature and technology to reduce CO₂ emissions along major city roads. Our pilot project focuses on transforming the Paris ring road into a living carbon sink using green infrastructure.

Our Mission

To make urban mobility more sustainable by capturing CO₂ emissions directly at their source through a hybrid system of vegetation, bio-technology, and community-based innovation.

Our Company Values

  • Environmental Responsibility – We prioritize nature-based solutions that regenerate the urban environment.
  • Social Inclusion – We promote green job creation and local community engagement.
  • Transparency & Innovation – We believe in open data, continuous monitoring, and research-driven impact.

Our ESG Commitments

1. Environmental – On-site CO₂ Capture

We use Miscanthus, Paulownia trees, and experimental bio-reactors to absorb CO₂ emissions along traffic-heavy corridors. These installations are designed to capture up to 12,000 tons of CO₂ per year while improving air quality and reducing urban heat islands.

2. Social – Green Jobs & Urban Regeneration

EcoLane creates inclusive employment opportunities in urban agriculture, environmental maintenance, and sustainable logistics. We also support a circular economy by transforming biomass into compost, biofuel, or timber.

3. Governance – Measurable, Transparent Impact

We deploy environmental sensors and publish real-time data on air quality and carbon capture. Our reports are independently reviewed, and we collaborate with research institutions to validate our results and ensure long-term accountability.
