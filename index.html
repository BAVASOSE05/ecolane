EcoLane

“Rethinking the road to breathe easier”

Our Vision

EcoLane started with a simple idea: we realised that road traffic is responsible for a major proportion of CO₂ emissions in Europe. In the Paris region, it accounts for more than half. Although the use of electric vehicles is gaining ground, they are not enough to respond to the climate emergency. So we need to act now. That's why we came up with the idea of EcoLane.

We started with the idea that roads could do more than just transport cars. At EcoLane, we believe that they can also make an active contribution to purifying the air. Our project transforms the Paris ring road into an ecological corridor. By combining nature and technology, we want to give the city of Paris a quality of life that matches its beauty.

Our Mission

  • Plantations of miscanthus and dwarf paulownia, selected for their rapid growth, low maintenance and exceptional ability to capture CO₂.
  • Absorbent foam blocks, integrated into lane separators and verges, capable of retaining up to 1,000 tonnes of CO₂ per year for 250 units installed.
  • Bioreactors installed on the central medians, transforming the CO₂ captured into green energy or recoverable biomass.

Project Progress

  • R&D phase (3 years): plant selection, material design and initial environmental simulations.
  • Field tests (2 years): validation of safety, effectiveness and urban integration.
  • Installation (1 year): covering 35 km of the Paris ring road (~15 ha of vegetated surfaces).

Our ESG Commitments

Environment

The main aim is to absorb CO₂ on the ring road—urban transport being a top contributor to global warming. By installing miscanthus, foam blocks and bioreactors, we can absorb 12 000 tonnes of CO₂ per year (≈10 % of ring road emissions) and convert part of it into green energy. EcoLane promises better air quality and living conditions for Parisians.

Social

We create jobs locally, support small farmers and nurseries, and subcontract maintenance to regional agencies—reinforcing local economic impact.

Governance

Full transparency: all data and results are shared with residents. No hidden figures, just facts.

Internal Structure & Team

EcoLane will employ 40–50 people on site (engineers, biotech experts, landscape architects, data analysts, coordination staff), plus partnerships with nurseries and maintenance companies.

Workspace & Organisation

Our teams work in eco-responsible buildings near our infrastructure. We promote well-being, collaboration, flexible practices and merit-based remuneration to foster performance and retention.

Project Development

  • R&D: €2–3 M
  • Deployment: ~€5 M
  • Annual maintenance: <€0.5 M

ROI expected in 6–8 years via carbon credits, subsidies and model exportability.

Expansion

Pending results on the Paris ring road, EcoLane peut être déployé dans toute grande ville au climat favorable à la croissance de miscanthus et paulownia.

PESTLE Analysis

  • Policy: soutien des autorités et politique favorable à la transition énergétique.
  • Economic: création d’emplois locaux, retour sur investissement rapide et exportabilité.
  • Social: qualité de vie améliorée et inclusion économique.
  • Technological: bioreacteurs et blocs absorbants à la pointe, modulaires et adaptables.
  • Environmental: réduction directe des émissions de CO₂ et amélioration de la qualité de l’air.
  • Legislative: conformité aux normes et régulations locales.

Conclusion

Projet évolutif et adaptable, avec à terme photovoltaïque et matériaux acoustiques pour un confort accru.

EcoLane: for a greener city, more useful roads and cleaner air.
